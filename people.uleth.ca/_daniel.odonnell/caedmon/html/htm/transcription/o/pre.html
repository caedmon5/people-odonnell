Poem

    Nu sculan[1] herıan      heofon|rıces ƿeard

    metodes mıhte      ond hıs mod〈g〉eþonc|

    ƿero[2] ƿuldorfæder      sƿa he ƿundra gehƿæs|

    ece dryhten      oór[3] onstealde

5     he ærest gesceop|      eorðan bearnunum[4]

    heofon to hrofe      halıg| scyppend

    ða mıddongeard      moncynnes ƿeard|

    ece dryhten      æfter teodde[5]

    fırum folda      frea[6]| ælmıhtıg·

Notes

[1]Nu sculan] ƿe added above the line with caret between Nu and sculan.

[2]ƿero] <o> corrected to <a> by overwriting.

[3]oór] <d> added above the line with caret after <r>.

[4]bearnunum] Word is followed by erasure of two or three letters. The original reading appears to have been bearnunum with <um> subsequently erased and the <n> changed to <m>.

[5]teodde] With erasure between <o> and final <d>.

[6]folda frea|] <n> added above the line with caret between folda and frea.
