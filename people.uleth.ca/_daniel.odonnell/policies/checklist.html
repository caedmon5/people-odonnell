Pre-Submission Checklist

Please print this checklist off an hand a completed copy in with your essay

You can use the following list to check that your essay meets the minimum requirements for your course. I will deduct a minimum of 1/3 of a letter grade from essays that do not meet applicable standards set out here; in serious cases I reserve the right to assign a greater penalty. Most of the points mentioned here are discussed in greater detail below. If you do not understand what a particular standard means, you should consult the MLA Handbook, Chicago Manual of Style, relevant sections of this web-site, or come and see me.

Layout (Click here for more information on layout and style):

My essay

  • has a title page
  • has a bibliography/works cited page (as necessary)
  • is typed/computer printed on one side of the page
  • uses an 11 or 12 point Roman-style font
  • is double spaced in the main body
  • has margins of 3cm all round
  • has page numbers on all pages except the title page

Quotations (Click here for more information on layout and style):

The quotations I use

  • are in-line or offset [i.e. "block"] as appropriate (See the MLA Handbook or Chicago Manual of Style for detailed instructions)
  • reproduce the text I am quoting from accurately
  • are accompanied by a correct and complete citation of source (See the MLA Handbook or Chicago Manual of Style for detailed instructions)

Documentation (Click here for more information on what and how to document):

  • The sources of all ideas and information that are not the product of my own research are documented.
  • My documentation style conforms to the guidelines in the MLA Handbook or Chicago Manual of Style

Proof-Reading

  • I have double-checked my essay for spelling mistakes, missing words, incomplete or problematic sentences.
  • I have double-checked all quotations and paraphrases.
